Redundant Router in a VM

Virtual Router Solves Redundancy Challenge for Large Enterprise

A client with a substantial workforce of over 3000 employees relied on a single Cisco ISR router, exposing them to significant network downtime risks. Budget constraints prevented the purchase of a redundant physical router.

To mitigate this risk, Wren Seven Techno Solutions implemented a Vyatta virtual router within a virtual machine. This innovative solution provided essential redundancy without incurring additional hardware costs.

By successfully deploying a virtual router, we ensured uninterrupted network operations for a large enterprise, demonstrating our ability to deliver cost-effective and reliable solutions.
