Saving huge Cost to a wind & Solar Energy Client

 

A renewable energy client faced the challenge of connecting over 50 remote Solar LDCs (Local Distribution Centers) across a vast geographical area. Traditional networking solutions would have incurred costs exceeding INR $ 1 million Us dolloars, a budget far exceeding the client's available funds (5%).

 

Wren Seven Techno Solutions engineered a cost-effective solution by implementing a carefully designed IP address schema and utilizing basic network equipment. This approach resulted in a stable and reliable network that continues to operate successfully even to this day
